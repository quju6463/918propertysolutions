About 918 Property Solutions

Builder knowledge
meets financial discipline.

918 Property Solutions brings together two practical sides of property renovation: trade experience from a qualified builder, and financial discipline from a chartered accountant.

We help homeowners and investors plan renovation work properly, manage project costs carefully, and focus on improvements that add real value.

Because we also do property projects ourselves, we think about budgets, resale value, rental potential and risk in a practical, hands-on way.

Renovation decision support

Know whether the work
is worth doing.

Before money goes into a project, we compare the practical outcomes: improve and sell, improve and rent, hold, short-stay, buy, or walk away.

01

Scope the work

Identify the repairs, presentation work and value-add improvements that should be considered first.

02

Build the budget

Test renovation cost, contingency, holding costs, selling costs, GST/tax assumptions and likely timing.

03

Check resale value

Compare likely resale uplift against project cost so the renovation is not driven by hope alone.

04

Check rental value

Look at rent range, tenant appeal, maintenance, insurance, rates and whether the property should be held.

05

Compare sale options

Decide whether to sell as-is, improve first, keep the property, or consider a direct purchase conversation.

06

Make the next move

Use the numbers and project plan to decide whether to renovate, rent, sell, buy, hold or walk away.

How it works

Send the property.
We shape the plan.

The aim is not to overwhelm you with theory. It is to turn the renovation or property decision into a few practical next steps.

1

Send the property details

Address or suburb, photos or listing link, current use, condition, rough numbers and what decision you are trying to make.

2

We test the project

Scope, budget, renovation risk, likely resale or rental outcome, hold costs and sale options, using the assumptions available.

3

You choose the next step

That might be renovating, selling, renting, using the calculators, getting specialist advice, or discussing a direct offer.

Clear interests

We also do property projects ourselves.

That experience helps us think practically about renovation, project cost, resale, holding costs and risk. It also means transparency matters. If we are interested in buying, partnering on, or being commercially involved in a property, we will say so clearly. In that situation, we are acting as a potential purchaser or project participant, not as your independent adviser. You should get your own legal, tax, valuation, finance or licensed real estate advice where needed.
